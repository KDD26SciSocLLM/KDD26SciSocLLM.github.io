**[Jian Tang](https://jian-tang.com/)** is an associate professor at Mila-Quebec AI Institute and HEC Montreal, with research interests in geometric deep learning, generative models (e.g., diffusion and flow matching) for drug discovery and protein design, and large language models for multiomics;. Prior to that, he was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Michigan and Carnegie Mellon University, and between 2014–2016 he worked as an Associate Researcher at Microsoft Research Asia.
