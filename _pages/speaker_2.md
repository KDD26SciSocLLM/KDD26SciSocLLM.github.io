**[Wang-Cheng Kang](https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~wckang/)** is a senior research engineer at Google DeepMind, with research interests in recommender systems and deep learning. He earned his Ph.D. from UC San Diego and previously worked with the LAMDA Group at Nanjing University. He also served as a postdoctoral researcher at Stanford’s Mobilize Center and held research internships at Google Brain (2019), Pinterest Labs (2018), and Adobe Research (2017). His work has been recognized with several honors, including a Best Paper Runner-up award at ACM RecSys 2017 and a Powell Fellowship at UC San Diego.
